About

"Financial failure is an education problem, not a character problem."

Banking background. Classroom experience teaching kids about money through Junior Achievement Finance Park. A founder who built the product because she lived the gap.

I believe most people don't fail financially because they're irresponsible — they fail because nobody ever taught them how it actually works. The rules, the tradeoffs, the compounding consequences. That's what I'm fixing: a gamified financial literacy app that meets people where they are and teaches them before the stakes hit.
